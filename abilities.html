Abilities Preview





Passive - Rising Spell Force


INNATE: Ezreal generates a stack of Rising Spell Force for each enemy hit by his abilities, which lasts 6 seconds, refreshes on subsequent hits and stacks up to 5 times.

RISING SPELL FORCE: For each stack, Ezreal gains Attack speed 10% bonus attack speed, up to a maximum of 50%.



Q - Mystic Shot


ACTIVE: Ezreal fires an orb in the target direction that marks the first enemy Champion champion, epic Monster monster, or structure hit for 4 seconds.

His next basic attack or ability against the target detonates the mark, dealing them magic damage.

MAGIC DAMAGE: 80 / 135 / 190 / 245 / 300 (+ 60% bonus AD) (+ 70 / 75 / 80 / 85 / 90% AP) If the mark was detonated with an ability, Ezreal restores the Mana mana cost of that ability plus Mana 60 mana.



W - Essence Flux


ACTIVE: Ezreal fires an orb in the target direction that marks the first enemy Champion champion, epic Monster monster, or structure hit for 4 seconds.

His next basic attack or ability against the target detonates the mark, dealing them magic damage.

MAGIC DAMAGE: 80 / 135 / 190 / 245 / 300 (+ 60% bonus AD) (+ 70 / 75 / 80 / 85 / 90% AP) If the mark was detonated with an ability, Ezreal restores the Mana mana cost of that ability plus Mana 60 mana.



E - Arcane Shift


ACTIVE: After the cast time, Ezreal blinks from his current location up-to 475 units towards the target location, firing a homing bolt that deals magic damage to the nearest enemy and briefly Reveal reveals them.

The bolt does not require the target to be Sight visible. Arcane Shift will cast at max range if cast beyond that.

MAGIC DAMAGE: 80 / 130 / 180 / 230 / 280 (+ 50% bonus AD) (+ 75% AP) Arcane Shift prioritizes targets hit by Essence Flux Essence Flux.



R - Trueshot Barrage


ACTIVE: Ezreal fires an energy projectile in the target direction, which briefly grants Sight sight of the area it flies through and deals magic damage to enemies hit.

MAGIC DAMAGE: 350 / 500 / 650 (+ 100% bonus AD) (+ 90% AP) Trueshot Barrage Minion Minions and non-epic Monster monsters take 50% reduced damage.

REDUCED DAMAGE: 175 / 250 / 325 (+ 50% bonus AD) (+ 45% AP)
